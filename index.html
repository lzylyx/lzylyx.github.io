I am currently working as a senior machine vision algorithm developer in a mobile robot company, and have been working on computer vision and image video algorithms for 4 years. My earliest contact with relevant work started from the recognition of DQ two-dimensional code in the undergraduate graduation program. After graduation, I have been engaged in the development of face recognition algorithms and computer vision algorithms.

The main achievements in recent years are:

  • In 2019, I developed the automatic meter recognition algorithm system of robot operating system.   • In 2017, We used fluorite camera to develop a face recognition algorithm, and the face recognition precise reached 95%.
